
   Product Specification
==========================

Use cases:
 (*) Encrypt & decrypt files on file system
 (*) Act as a secured "bank" of data - securely store data
 (*) Manage accounts for different users


More specific requirements: 
 1. Account management
   1.1. Useer can delete account - will delete all users' data as well
 2. Encryption and decryption of data *must* be based on password alone (password will be the seed of encryption)
